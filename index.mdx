## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Collabase">
    Unzip the deployment package you received from Collabase and run the installer:
    ```bash
    bash install.sh
    ```
    It will ask for your URL, admin email, and password — then do the rest automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in and set your password">
    Open your Collabase URL in a browser and log in with the credentials you set during installation. You will be asked to choose a new password on your first login.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first Space">
    A Space is the container for your team's work — wiki, tasks, test cases, and automations all live inside a Space. Create one for your first project or department.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Go to **Admin → Users** and add your colleagues. Assign them to Spaces with the role that matches what they need to do.
  </Step>
</Steps>
