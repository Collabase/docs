Collabase is a self-hosted collaboration suite that keeps your data on your own servers. It combines a rich-text knowledge base, a quality assurance test management system, a visual automation builder, and an internal communication hub — all in one platform with a shared permission model and unified search.

## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Collabase">
    Run the one-line installer on any Linux, macOS, or Windows (WSL2) machine with Docker installed.
    ```bash
    curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/briantrp/collabase/main/deployment/install.sh | bash
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the setup wizard">
    Open your browser, follow the setup wizard to initialize the database, and create your admin account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first Space">
    Spaces are the top-level containers for your team's work. Create one for a project, department, or product area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start collaborating">
    Add pages, run tests, build automations, and invite your team — everything is in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>
