# Intranet

<img src="/images/intranet.png" alt="Collabase Intranet feed" style={{ borderRadius: '8px', marginBottom: '24px' }} />

The Intranet is your organization's internal communication hub. It aggregates company announcements, events, blog posts, quick access links, and team birthdays into a single feed — accessible to everyone in the organization.

## What's in the Intranet

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Company Feed" icon="rss" href="/intranet/blog-posts">
    Published blog posts from all Spaces appear in a chronological company-wide feed. Filter by Space or author.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Events" icon="calendar" href="/intranet/events">
    Upcoming company events, team meetings, and important dates visible to everyone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Quick Links" icon="link" href="/intranet/quick-links">
    Pinned shortcuts to frequently used tools, documents, and external resources.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Birthdays" icon="cake-candles" href="/intranet/birthdays">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who sees the Intranet

All authenticated Collabase users can access the Intranet at the `/intranet` route. Content visibility respects Space permissions — blog posts from private Spaces are only shown to members of that Space.

## Admin configuration

Admins control the Intranet's layout and branding under **Admin → Intranet Config**:

- Set a welcome message or banner image
- Enable or disable the birthday widget
- Configure quick links available to all users (users can add personal quick links too)
- Control which Spaces contribute to the company feed
