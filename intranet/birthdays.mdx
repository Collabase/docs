# Birthdays

The Birthdays widget on the Intranet home shows upcoming team birthdays for the next 30 days, providing a simple way to recognize and celebrate teammates.

## How it works

1. Team members who want to be recognized add their birthday to their **Profile → Personal → Birthday**
2. The birthday widget on the Intranet home shows names and dates for upcoming birthdays
3. On a team member's birthday, they appear at the top of the widget with a celebration indicator 🎂

## Privacy

- Birthdays are **opt-in** — the widget only shows people who have explicitly set their birthday in their profile
- The year of birth is never shown — only month and day
- Users can remove their birthday from their profile at any time and they'll be removed from the widget immediately

## Admin configuration

Admins can disable the birthday widget entirely under **Admin → Intranet Config → Display → Show Birthdays Widget**.
