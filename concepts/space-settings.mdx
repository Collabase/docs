title: "Space settings"
description: "Configure your Collabase Space settings — rename the space, enable or disable apps, manage member roles, and transfer ownership or delete the space."
---

Space settings let Space Owners and Space Admins configure how a Space looks, what apps are active in it, and who has access. Open them by navigating to your Space and clicking **Settings** in the sidebar.

---

## General

The **General** tab controls the Space's identity.

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Name** | The display name shown in the sidebar and header. Can be changed at any time. |
| **Mission statement** | A short description shown on the Space cockpit. Helps members understand what the Space is for at a glance. |
| **Icon** | An emoji or icon displayed next to the Space name. |
| **Visibility** | Controls who can see and access the Space — `Private`, `Internal`, or `Public`. See [Spaces](/concepts/spaces) for details. |

<Warning>
  The Space **slug** is set at creation time and cannot be changed afterwards. All Collabase links use the slug, so changing it would break existing URLs.
</Warning>

---

## Apps

The **Apps** tab lets you enable or disable Collabase apps within this Space. All apps are enabled by default when a Space is created.

| App | What it does when enabled |
|---|---|
| **Docs** | Members can create and manage pages, templates, and the page tree |
| **Test Management** | Members can create test projects, suites, cases, runs, and record results |
| **Automation** | Members can build and run automation pipelines |
| **Intranet** | Members can publish announcements and blog posts |

Disabling an app hides it from the sidebar for all members. Existing data is preserved — re-enabling the app restores access to it.

## Members

The **Members** tab lets you invite individual users or groups and assign their Space role.

### Inviting a member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Space Settings → Members">
    Click the **Add Member** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for the user">
    Type a name or email address. Only users who already have a Collabase account are shown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a Space role">
    Choose **Owner**, **Admin**, **Member**, or **Viewer**. See [Permissions](/concepts/permissions) for what each role can do.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add">
    The user immediately gains access to the Space with the selected role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Granting access to a Group

Instead of inviting individual users, you can grant access to an entire Group at once. All current and future members of the Group inherit the Space role you assign.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Group in the Members tab">
    Select the Group from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a Space role">
    Choose the role for all members of this Group.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Save">
    Group membership is reflected immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Changing or removing a member

- To change a member's role, use the role dropdown on their row.
- To remove a member, click the remove button on their row. The user immediately loses access to the Space.

## Danger zone

The **Danger zone** tab contains irreversible actions.

| Action | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Delete Space** | Permanently deletes the Space and all its content — pages, test projects, automations, and member assignments. This cannot be undone. |

<Warning>
  Deleting a Space is permanent. Export or back up any critical content before proceeding. Only the Space Owner can delete a Space.
</Warning>
