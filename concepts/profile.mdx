title: "Your profile"
description: "Update your personal profile, change your password, and manage your notification preferences."
---

Your profile controls how you appear to other members in Collabase and how you receive notifications. All profile settings are personal — they only affect your own account.

Open your profile by clicking your **avatar** in the bottom-left corner of the sidebar and selecting **Profile**.

---

## Personal information

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Display name** | The name that appears next to your content and in member lists. |
| **Email** | Your login email address. Used for password reset and notifications. |
| **Phone** | Optional — shown in your profile card when others click your name. |
| **Job title** | Optional — shown in your profile card. |
| **Department** | Optional — used in member directories and filter views. |
| **Avatar** | A profile picture shown next to your name throughout the app. Upload a new one by clicking your current avatar. |

Click **Save** after making any changes.

<Note>
  If your account was provisioned by an identity provider (LDAP, SAML, or SCIM), your name, email, and department fields may be locked. Changes to those fields must be made in your company directory — they will sync to Collabase on the next login or directory sync.
</Note>

---

## Changing your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile">
    Click your avatar → **Profile**, then select the **Security** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your current password">
    You must provide your current password to confirm your identity before setting a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter and confirm the new password">
    Your new password must be at least 8 characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Save">
    Your password is updated immediately. All existing sessions remain active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Password change is not available for accounts that sign in exclusively through SSO (Google, Microsoft, SAML, etc.). Contact your administrator to manage those credentials through your identity provider.
</Note>

## Notification preferences

Collabase sends in-app notifications when events relevant to you occur — for example, when an automation notifies you, when someone mentions you in a page, or when a test run you started completes.

Open **Profile → Notifications** to configure when and how you receive them.

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **In-app notifications** | Controls the notification bell in the top bar. Always on — cannot be disabled. |
| **Email digest** | Sends a summary of unread notifications to your email address. Choose **Off**, **Daily**, or **Weekly**. |

### Viewing and dismissing notifications

Click the **bell icon** in the top bar to open the notification panel. Unread notifications are highlighted. Click any notification to navigate to the relevant item and mark it as read. Click **Mark all as read** to clear all unread indicators at once.
